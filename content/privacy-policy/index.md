This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is collected, used, and protected when you visit **tabular.com**, use forms on the site, schedule calls, purchase training tickets, communicate with Tabular B.V., exercise GDPR rights, or participate in optional community channels.

## 1) Contact

- **General enquiries:** https://forms.tabular.com/contact  
- **Data rights requests:** https://forms.tabular.com/drrf  

**Data Protection Officer (DPO):** Chris Woolderink.

## 2) Personal data collected

### 2.1 Forms hosted by YouForm (contact, signup, Data Rights Requests)
We use YouForm for:
- Contact forms  
- Signup forms  
- **Data Rights Request Form (DRRF)**  
- Other forms with free-text fields  

Depending on the form, we may collect:
- Name  
- Email address  
- Phone number (optional)  
- Organisation (optional)  
- **Free-text messages or information you provide voluntarily**  
- Data required to process GDPR rights requests  

YouForm stores submissions and forwards them to our email inbox.  
Submissions are **not automatically deleted**.

### 2.2 Scheduling calls (Cal.com)
When scheduling meetings via Cal.com, we collect:
- Name  
- Email address  
- Additional notes (optional)

Cal.com shares this information with us so the meeting can be held and prepared.

### 2.3 Ticket sales and payments (Tito + Stripe)
When purchasing training tickets via Tito:
- Name  
- Email address  
- Company / organisation  
- VAT number  
- Billing address (street, city, region, postal code, country)  
- Ticket and invoice details  

Payments are processed securely by **Stripe**.  
We **never receive or store** your full card details.

Refunds and chargebacks occur within Tito/Stripe without additional personal data collection.

### 2.4 Course‑related communications
Training participants receive required service communications (e.g., logistics, updates).  
These are essential and cannot be unsubscribed from.

### 2.5 Slack community (optional)
For optional membership in the **“TABULAR developers”** Slack workspace, we use:
- Name  
- Email address  

Participation is optional and can be ended at any time.

### 2.6 Analytics (Umami Cloud)
We use Umami Cloud with:
- **Cookieless mode**  
- **IP anonymization**  

No cookies or full IP addresses are stored.

## 4) Legal bases for processing

### **Contract**
- Ticket purchases

- Training delivery  
- Meeting scheduling

### **Legitimate interests**
- Responding to enquiries  
- Handling Data Rights Requests

### **Consent**
Used for truly optional or voluntary information (e.g., free-text notes).

## 5) Cookies
- Tabular B.V. does **not** set cookies.  
- Umami Cloud uses **cookieless analytics**.  
- Third‑party services may use cookies based on their own policies.

## 6) Sharing of data

We only share personal data with essential service providers:

- **YouForm** – hosting and delivery of form submissions  
- **Cal.com** – scheduling information  
- **Tito** – ticketing and invoicing  
- **Stripe** – payment processing  
- **Email provider** – communications delivery  
- **Slack** – optional community workspace  

All providers follow their own privacy and security standards.

## 7) Hosting and logs

The site uses **Hugo** and is hosted via **GitHub Pages**.  
GitHub may process limited technical logs for security and performance.

## 8) International transfers

Some providers may process data outside the EU/EEA.  
When this occurs, they apply legally recognised safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

## 9) Security

We apply reasonable organisational and technical measures, including encrypted transmission (TLS) and limited-access controls.

## 10) Your rights

You may exercise GDPR rights:

- Access  
- Rectification  
- Erasure  
- Restriction  
- Portability  
- Objection  
- Withdrawal of consent (when processing is based on consent)  

Submit requests using:  
**https://forms.tabular.com/drrf**

You may also contact the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens).

## 11) Children  
Our services are **not intended for children under 16**, and we do not knowingly collect their data.
